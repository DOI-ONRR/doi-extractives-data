Total revenues by location

These two datasets contain revenue information for calendar year 2013. It is organized by location and is {% for term in site.data.terms %} {% if term.term == "data accounting" %} accounting year {% endif %} {% endfor %} data. The offshore data is organized by offshore planning areas and the onshore data is organized by state. A subset of this data is what powers the map interactive display on this site's homepage.

These datasets contain information on the following commodities:

  • Oil. Includes various crude oil products such as condensate, drip and scrubber condensate, and fuel oil.
  • Gas. Includes various natural gas products such as processed and unprocessed gas, natural gas liquids (NGL), nitrogen, flash gas, fuel gas, and coal bed methane.
  • Coal. Includes various processed and unprocessed coal products such as anthracite, bituminous, subbituminous, and lignite.
  • Other. Includes a wide variety of solid mineral and other products such as copper, zinc, tar sands, clay, borax, and potassium.
  • Wind. from offshore wind turbine projects. These leases have not started producing in paying quanitities so there are no royalties yet.
  • Geothermal. Includes royalties from geothermal electrical generation and the resulting byproducts.
