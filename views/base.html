The Department of the Interior is creating this public data portal to tell the story of natural resource revenues from Federal lands.

It supports the U.S. implementation of the international Extractive Industry Transparency Initiative, is part of the cross-government Project Open Data and supports the goals of the U.S. National Action Plan for Open Government Partnership.

Questions?

Contact the Department of the Interior's Office of Natural Resources Revenue about natural resource revenues at useiti@ios.doi.gov.
